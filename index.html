Scott's Bio

Scott Davis is a certified PMP, Scrum Master, Scrum at Scale, Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Program Consultant (SP5), Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, ITIL Foundations, and Business Process Management Professional. He is a Technology Manager for LTS and works in support of the Veteranâ€™s Administration Office of Information and Technology. Over the last two years, Scott has been learning full-stack web development, first through numerous MOOC courses and now through the MIT MERN Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. Before working at LTS, he was the Senior Business Process Analyst for the eCommerce group at Belk Department stores.

Scott, a retired Army officer (2011), was a brigade executive officer and logistics planner. Immediately after retiring, he worked as a contractor for Science Applications International Corp. (SAIC) in Afghanistan with the Joint Program Office (JPO) for Mine Resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP) vehicles as a lead planner. He was president of the local Project Management Institute chapter from 2016-2017 and has presented at various Agile and Project Management events. Scott has an undergraduate degree in Marketing from the University of Colorado, an M.Div. from Princeton Seminary, and an MBA from Webster University.
